How technology has affected Us?

According to statics, there are more than 5.1 billion active users on the internet. For better comprehension, 6 out of 10 people in the world are online users and with the advancements in technology, that number is only bound to grow. With these numbers, in bigger parts of the world, life without technology is difficult to fathom. Even a momentary breach of the internet or the power can cause irreplaceable damage to businesses and personal life. Technology has become an integral part of life and business.

Minitsu's Origin

In the 90s, japan went through devastating economic turmoil and a recession. While the turmoil (so-called the Lost Decades) would end in the 2000s, many unemployed salary workers had to find different lines of work. During this period, a small group of political enthusiasts in Kyoto japan got together occasionally to share their love of rearing and taking care of small trees called bonsai, and Minitsu was created.
