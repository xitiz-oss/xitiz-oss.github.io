Research

For our website inspiration, we not only looked at bonsai-based websites but websites from all kinds of organisations. From a website that sells furniture to a website for clothing, we did not shy away from taking inspiration from where we can find them to make our website more userfriendly and to make it look clean.

Links mentioned:

  1. GEORGE NAKASHIMA WOODWORKERS
  2. WOOVEN MAGAZINE
  3. BONSAI EMPIRE
  4. PLANT WORLD LONDON
  5. CLOUD HORIZON

1. GEORGE NAKASHIMA WOODWORKERS

We wanted a a simple and elegant look for out home page. Therefore, we took inspiration from the nakashimawoodworkers.com/. Our team really liked the clean look the website implemented with minimalistic aesthetics so we decided to implement a similar design.

We also took an inspiration from the website's nav bar which changed to transparant after scrolling down. Therefore, we implemented the design for our website so the navbar appears everytime the user scrool downs to view other content of the website.

2. WOOVEN MAGAZINE

The woovenmagazine’s website features a simple design with minimalistic colours. But the thing that stood out the most to us was the carousel that was implemented to show different blogs that were available to read on the website. Inspired by it, we decided to implement our own carousel using css and javascript, which showed the history of bonsai.

3. BONSAI EMPIRE

From the bonsai empire, we got all the information about our bonsai species available along with the inspiration for the blog post link. We also got the idea to implement the newsletter feature at the footer to ask the users to join our newsletter.

4. PLANT WORLD LONDON

Plant world London’s website was a huge inspiration for us in terms of the individual products page. It had the elegant and clean look we were striving for. Therefore, for all of our products, we decided to implement a simpler aesthetic.

5. CLOUD HORIZON

For the about us page, we decided to implement Cloud Horizon’s “Meet Our Team” section on the home page. We really liked the classy square designs and symmetric aesthetics which also fit perfectly with our home page design.
